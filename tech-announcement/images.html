The new Gear VR headset works with the Galaxy S8 Plus, S8, S7, S7 Edge, Note5, S6, S6 Edge and S6 Edge+ and comes with Micro USB and USB-C adapters.The larger Gear VR, while bulkier and more plastic, fits better on most faces. The Gear VR still works with wired headphones, leaving the headphone jack space on the phone unobstructed. But it's arguably better to use wireless headphones to reduce the cable tangle. To be clear, this isn't the same high-end VR hardware as the HTC Vive or Oculus Rift. Those are more-advanced pieces of tech that also require a cutting-edge PC.
